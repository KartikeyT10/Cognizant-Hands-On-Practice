# Exercise 1: Control Structures

## Scenario 1: Apply Discount to Loan Interest Rates

### Observation
The block checks the age of each customer and reduces the loan interest rate by 1% for customers older than 60 years.

---

## Scenario 2: Promote Customers to VIP Status

### Observation
The block iterates through all customers and marks customers with a balance greater than $10,000 as VIP customers.

---

## Scenario 3: Loan Due Reminders

### Sample Output
`	ext
Reminder: Loan 101 for Customer 1 is due on 15-JUN-25
Reminder: Loan 102 for Customer 2 is due on 20-JUN-25
`

### Observation
The block retrieves loans due within the next 30 days and prints a reminder message for each customer.

## Conclusion
This exercise demonstrates the use of PL/SQL control structures such as loops, conditional statements, and cursor-based iteration to perform banking operations efficiently.
